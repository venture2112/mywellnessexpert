Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, treatments, and approach to functional wellness.

General Questions

What makes functional wellness different from regular chiropractic care?

+

Traditional chiropractic care focuses primarily on spinal adjustments to address musculoskeletal issues. Functional wellness takes a comprehensive approach that looks at your entire body system—including nutrition, lifestyle, stress levels, and overall health patterns. We don't just treat symptoms; we identify and address the root causes of your health challenges using advanced diagnostic tools and therapeutic technologies.

Do I need a referral to see Dr. Asakura?

+

No referral is necessary. You can schedule an appointment directly. However, if you have recent imaging (X-rays, MRI, CT scans) or lab work from other providers, please bring them to your first appointment as they can help us understand your health history better.

What should I expect during my first visit?

+

Your initial consultation is comprehensive and typically lasts 60-90 minutes. We'll discuss your health history, current symptoms, lifestyle factors, and wellness goals. Dr. Asakura will perform a physical examination and may recommend additional testing. By the end of the visit, you'll have a clear understanding of what's causing your health issues and a personalized treatment plan.

How long before I see results?

+

Results vary depending on your condition, its severity, and how long you've been experiencing symptoms. Many patients report noticeable improvement within 2-4 weeks, while chronic conditions may take 2-3 months for significant change. During your consultation, Dr. Asakura will give you a realistic timeline based on your specific situation.

Treatments & Services

What is BrainHealthPlus and who is it for?

+

BrainHealthPlus is a revolutionary brain training program that uses neurofeedback technology to improve cognitive function, memory, and balance. It's particularly beneficial for seniors concerned about maintaining independence, athletes looking to enhance performance, and anyone experiencing brain fog or cognitive decline. The program is covered by Medicare for qualifying patients.

Is shockwave therapy painful?

+

Most patients describe shockwave therapy as uncomfortable but not painful. You may feel a tapping or pulsing sensation during treatment. The intensity is adjusted to your comfort level, and sessions are relatively brief (typically 10-15 minutes). Any discomfort usually subsides immediately after treatment, and many patients experience pain relief within 24-48 hours.

How does red light therapy work?

+

Red light therapy uses specific wavelengths of red and near-infrared light to penetrate deep into tissues, where it stimulates cellular energy production (ATP), reduces inflammation, and promotes healing. It's completely non-invasive, painless, and has been shown to improve skin health, reduce joint pain, accelerate muscle recovery, and support overall cellular function.

What conditions can decompression therapy help with?

+

Decompression therapy is beneficial for a wide range of conditions including poor circulation, lymphedema, chronic venous insufficiency, post-surgical swelling, sports recovery, and general wellness. It enhances blood flow, reduces fluid accumulation, and supports your body's natural healing processes.

Safety & Effectiveness

Are your treatments safe for seniors?

+

Absolutely. Our treatments are gentle and customized to each patient's needs and limitations. We've successfully helped patients from age 18 to 85, including those with osteoporosis, arthritis, and other age-related conditions. Safety and comfort are our top priorities, and we adjust all treatments accordingly.

Can I combine treatments or do I need to choose one?

+

Many of our patients benefit from a combination of treatments. In fact, we often recommend integrated protocols that might include chiropractic care, decompression therapy, and red light therapy working together. During your consultation, Dr. Asakura will recommend the optimal combination for your specific condition.

What if I've tried everything else and nothing has worked?

+

Many of our patients come to us after trying conventional treatments without success. Our functional wellness approach looks deeper than standard care, often identifying underlying issues that have been missed. We use advanced diagnostic tools and treat the whole person, not just symptoms. If you're frustrated with lack of progress elsewhere, we encourage you to schedule a consultation.
