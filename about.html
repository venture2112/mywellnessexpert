The Asakura Approach

Dr. Asakura's methodology goes far beyond traditional chiropractic adjustments. Her functional wellness approach integrates multiple disciplines to create personalized treatment plans that address your unique needs.

Every patient begins with a comprehensive assessment that examines not just your symptoms, but your lifestyle, nutrition, stress levels, and overall wellness patterns. This holistic view allows us to identify the true source of your health concerns.

Then, using state-of-the-art therapeutic technologies—including Electrical Stimulation, Compression Therapy, Red Light Cold Fusion, and Shockwave Therapy—we create a customized protocol designed to restore your body's natural healing capabilities.
