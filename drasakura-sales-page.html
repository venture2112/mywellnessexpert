Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Functional Wellness different from regular chiropractic care?

Traditional chiropractic focuses primarily on spinal adjustments. Functional Wellness looks at your entire body system—nutrition, movement, stress, sleep, and more. We use advanced technology and comprehensive testing to find the root cause of your issues, not just treat symptoms.

How long does a typical treatment plan last?

Every patient is different, but most people see significant improvement within 4-6 weeks. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss your specific situation and create a timeline tailored to your goals and condition severity.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans for chiropractic care. However, some of our advanced functional wellness services may not be covered. We'll provide a detailed cost breakdown during your consultation and work with you to maximize your benefits.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your initial consultation includes a comprehensive health history review, physical examination, and discussion of your goals. We'll use this information to create your personalized treatment plan. The visit typically lasts 45-60 minutes—much longer than a standard doctor's appointment.

Is this approach safe for seniors or people with chronic conditions?

Absolutely. Our treatments are gentle and customized to each patient's needs and limitations. We've successfully helped patients from age 18 to 85, including those with arthritis, osteoporosis, and other chronic conditions. Safety and comfort are our top priorities.
